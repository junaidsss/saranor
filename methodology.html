The Saranor Framework

Our methodology is engineered for precision, clarity, and measurable business impact — built around four structured phases.

1. Discover

We conduct a detailed diagnostic to understand workflows, data maturity, systems, and pain points across the organization.

  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Process mapping & workflow audits
  • Data quality & systems assessment
  • Opportunity identification
  • Pain-point analysis & prioritization

2. Define

Insights from discovery are shaped into strategy, requirements, and measurable outcomes. This is the alignment and planning phase.

  • Solution scoping & requirements
  • AI opportunity mapping
  • Data readiness & infrastructure planning
  • Success metrics & measurable outcomes
  • Implementation roadmap design

3. Design

We translate strategy into system blueprints: architecture, workflows, data flows, user experience, and adoption planning.

  • System & workflow architecture
  • Data pipelines & transformation mapping
  • Model & automation design
  • User experience & interface planning
  • Change management & adoption design

4. Deliver

Build, deploy, validate, and optimize — ensuring models, workflows, and systems deliver measurable outcomes and enterprise ROI.

  • Solution build & configuration
  • Model training, tuning & validation
  • Workflow automation deployment
  • User testing & feedback integration
  • Post-launch monitoring & continuous improvement
